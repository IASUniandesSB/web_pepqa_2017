Author Guidelines

2017 IEEE Workshop on Power Electronics and Power Quality Applications (PEPQA)

PEPQA 2017 is mainly concerned with disseminating completed and ongoing research in power quality and power electronics field. All papers submitted to PEPQA 2017 must be minimum of four and maximum six pages in English or Spanish. These studies must be original, relevant to current research and theoretical implications being scientifically rigorous and having great depth of analysis.
  1. Take a look at the topics on the call for papers: http://eepurl.com/czHRa9
    Papers are peer-reviewed and selected on the basis of technical quality, topic affinity, and applicability to the industry.

  2. Download the official IEEE conference templates (LaTeX and M. Word): http://www.ieee.org/conferences_events/conferences/publishing/templates.html

  3. Follow the IEEE style guidelines: https://www.ieee.org/documents/style_manual.pdf

  4. Be aware of the general requirements:
    1. Original and unpublished works only.
    2. Full paper submission.
    3. Minimum of four pages and a maximum of six pages.
    4. English or Spanish will be considered for publication.
  5. Do not miss the full paper submission deadline: March 13, 2017.

  6. Proceedings are going to be indexed by IEEE Xplore, and they will be submitted to SCOPUS and Thompson's Conference Proceedings Citation Index: The title and abstract must be in the English language.

  7. Top papers presented at this Conference will be eligible for review for inclusion in the IEEE Transactions on Industry Applications or the Industry Applications Magazine: Be ready with an excellent paper about your research.

  8. Submit the full paper PDF version with EasyChair: https://easychair.org/cfp/PEPQA2019 (tutorial: http://pepqa.com.co/resources/TutorialEasyChairPEPQA2019.pdf)
