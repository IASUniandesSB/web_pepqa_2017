Universidad Nacional de Colombia, Sede Manizales.

The National University of Colombia headquarters Manizales is a Colombian public university, headquarters of the National University of Colombia, located in the city of Manizales, Caldas.. Located next to the airport of the same name, in the former facilities of the workshops of the Department of Public Works of the Department of Caldas, has an area of 101,504 m² and about 21,760 m² of built area. There, the curricular programs of Chemical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Physical Engineering, Electronic Engineering, Industrial Engineering, Mathematics and Information Systems Administration work.
