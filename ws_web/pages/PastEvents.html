The PEPQA is an international event in which students, academic community and professionals meet each other to learn and share the most recent trends and challenges on Power Electronics (PE) and Power Quality (PQ). The purpose of this workshop is to contribute in the process of identification and solving problems in the growing industry of PE and PQ with activities directed by experts in areas such as power converters, speed drivers, power electronics in transmission and distribution systems, and modeling, design and simulation for power quality.

PEPQA is organized by a team of volunteers, members of the Uniandes SB IAS Chapter and the Colombian IAS Chapter. Attendees participate in keynotes sessions, tutorials, hands on, and paper presentations. The event has enjoyed the participation of Mauricio Aredes (from Universidad de Río de Janeiro), Mark McGranagham and Roger Dugan (from the Electric Power Research Institute, EPRI), and José Rodriguez (Andrés Bello National University, Santiago de Chile), among other distinguished speakers
